Squire

The RPG combat assistant.

Get it on Google Play
Coming Soon! Download APK
Beta 1.1

Squire is an Android application that keeps track of the current value of all your characters combat stats, as well as what buffs, debuffs, and other modifiers are currently affecting them.

Never forget another buff

Are you tired of missing your attack because you forgot that +1 buff? Are the other players getting annoyed at you having to add up all your different bonuses? Let Squire do the hard work instead, while you focus on the game.

System independent

Pathfinder, Dungeons & Dragons, GURPS, or any other system - as long as your system involves numbers, Squire can help you. Squire comes pre-loaded with some example stats and modifiers for Pathfinder, but it's easy to add your own stats and modifiers to make Squire work for you.

All kinds of free

Squire is free to download (gratis) and open source (libre). This means you can download and use the app, modify the source code, and basically do whatever you want.

Contact and feedback

Squire is an ongoing project, and I'd love to hear from you! If you have any feature requests, bug reports, or other suggestions, head over to the GitHub issues page. For anything else, email squire@cmrn.me.
